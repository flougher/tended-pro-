Introduction

Tended Health Pro ("the App," "we," "our") is a professional caregiver documentation tool developed for Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs), Home Health Aides (HHAs), and other direct care workers. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

By downloading or using Tended Health Pro, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

Developer: Tended Health Apps · Contact: see Section 10 below
2
How Your Data Is Stored

Tended Health Pro is a local-first application. All data you enter — including patient records, medication logs, incident reports, care plans, billing entries, sundowning episodes, wandering incidents, and memory care notes — is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage.

  • No data is transmitted to any server or cloud service
  • No data is shared with the developer or any third party
  • No internet connection is required to use the App
  • Data persists on your device between sessions automatically
  • Data is removed when you uninstall the App or clear app data
⚠️ Important: Because data is stored locally, it is not automatically backed up. We strongly recommend using the Export feature in Settings regularly to back up your data to a secure location.
3
Patient & Health Information

Tended Health Pro is designed to help caregivers document patient care. The App may be used to record Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA, including:

  • Patient names, dates of birth, and room numbers
  • Medical conditions, allergies, and medications
  • Incident reports, wound assessments, and behavior logs
  • Care plans, dietary records, and toileting logs
  • Sundowning episodes, wandering and elopement incidents
  • Memory care communication notes and cognitive observations
  • Family contact information

All such information is stored solely on your device and is never transmitted to, accessed by, or visible to the App developer or any third party. You are solely responsible for the security of your device and compliance with your employer's HIPAA policies.

HIPAA Notice: Tended Health Pro is a device-based documentation tool. Caregivers are responsible for ensuring their device meets their agency's security requirements and that they are authorized to record the information they enter.
4
Information We Collect

Tended Health Pro collects no personal information from users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or app behavior data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any content you enter into the App

The only data exchange that occurs is through Google Play's standard purchase verification when you purchase the app, which is handled entirely by Google and subject to Google's own Privacy Policy.

5
In-App Purchases & Billing

Tended Health Pro offers a one-time purchase to unlock full access ($14.99 USD) after a 10-day free trial. All purchase transactions are processed exclusively through Google Play Billing.

  • We do not collect, store, or process your payment information
  • All payment data is handled by Google Play — see Google's Privacy Policy
  • Purchase status is verified through Google Play and stored locally on your device
  • Refund requests must be submitted through Google Play
Restore Purchase: If you reinstall the App, use the "Restore Purchase" option in Settings to re-verify your purchase through Google Play.
6
Device Permissions

Tended Health Pro requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • Microphone — Used only for the Voice to Text feature when you explicitly tap the microphone button. Audio is processed on-device and is never recorded or transmitted.
  • Internet access — Required for Google Play purchase verification and to load the app font resources on first launch only.

The App does not request access to your camera, contacts, location, files, photos, or any other device resources.

7
Third-Party Services

Tended Health Pro uses a minimal set of third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing — For processing the one-time purchase. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Fonts — IBM Plex Sans and Playfair Display fonts are loaded from Google Fonts on first use. This involves a standard web request to Google's servers. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics services, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party SDKs.

8
Children's Privacy

Tended Health Pro is designed exclusively for use by professional adult caregivers (age 18+). The App is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has used this App and provided personal information, please contact us immediately at the address in Section 10 and we will take appropriate steps.

9
Your Rights & Data Control

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access — All your data is visible within the App at all times
  • Export — Use Settings → Export to download all your data as a JSON file
  • Delete — Clear all data by clearing the App's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App
  • Portability — Your exported JSON file can be imported into any future version of Tended Health Pro

Since we collect no personal data on our servers, there is no data to request deletion of from us directly.
